# AI Governance Overview

## Risk Classification

AI systems should be classified according to their potential impact, autonomy, and access to sensitive resources.

## High-Impact Agents

High-impact agents require human approval before consequential actions, detailed audit logs, restricted tool access, and continuous monitoring.

## Low-Risk Assistants

Low-risk internal assistants may operate with fewer controls when they cannot modify systems, access sensitive data, or perform external actions.

## Evidence and Accountability

Recommendations should distinguish retrieved evidence from inference and clearly report uncertainty and missing evidence.
