# Retrieval Evaluation

## Hit Rate

Retrieval Hit Rate at k measures whether the expected source appears among the top retrieved results.

## Context Relevance

Context relevance measures how directly the retrieved text supports the question.

## Claim Support

Claim support rate measures the percentage of factual claims supported by retrieved evidence.

## Chunking

Small chunks may improve precision but lose context. Large chunks may preserve context but include irrelevant information.
